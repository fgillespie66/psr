Recruitment
Frequently Asked Questions
How do I rush Phi Rho this semester (Fall 2020)?
This semester, the IFC (InterFraternity Council) has decided to postpone a formal bidding recruitment process until Spring 2021. This was decided so that every potential new member would have the opportunity to meet the Greek group in person before making an official commitment. However, instead of a formal rush, we will be hosting informal "recruitment" events so that potential new members can get to know us without any pressure of bids!

Is Phi Rho giving bids this semester (Fall 2020)?
Currently, no members of the IFC are giving official bids. However, come hang out at our informal events!

How does rush work in a typical semester?
In a typical semester, Phi Rho (along with the rest of the IFC) will have a Rush Week. This week will have informal events to provide an opportunity for potential new members to meet the members of Phi Rho! In the past, we have had a boba and board games event, a Project Runway Marathon, a card making service event, a sundae event, a trip to Boston, and more! To rush Phi Rho, come to at least 2 of our events!.

How can I learn more about what it means to be in Phi Rho?
You are welcome and encouraged to come to our informal events! For more specific information about our events, responsibilities, or anything else, please email psr-exec@mit.edu!

I have more questions about recruitment. Who should I contact?
For any questions, comments, or concerns about the recruitment process, please email psr-recruitment@mit.edu!
